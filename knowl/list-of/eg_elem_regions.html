Example 1.14.13. Start-shaped and elementary regions.

It is not difficult to show that the following regions are star-shaped:
  • \(\C-R_{\alpha}\text{,}\) \(\alpha\in \R\text{,}\)
  • \(\{z\in \C\colon \abs{z}> r, \pi/2-\alpha< \Arg_{0} z< \pi/2+\alpha\}\text{,}\) \(r> 0\text{,}\) \(0< \alpha < \pi/2\text{.}\)
  • \(\{z\in \C\colon r < \abs{z}< R, \pi/2-\alpha< \Arg_{0} z< \pi/2+\alpha\}\text{,}\)
