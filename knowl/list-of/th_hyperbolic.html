Theorem 1.7.11. Hyperbolic.

Let \(z\in \C\text{.}\)
  1. \(\cosh(z)=\cos(iz)\) and \(\sinh(z)=\frac{\sin(iz)}{i}\text{.}\)
  2. Writing \(z=x+iy\text{,}\) we have
    \begin{align*} \cos(z) \amp =\cos(x)\cosh(iy)-i\sin(x)\sinh(y)\\ \sin(z) \amp =\sin(x)\cosh(y)+i\cos(x)\sinh(y) \end{align*}

Proof.
