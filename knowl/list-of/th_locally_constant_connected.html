Theorem 1.9.15. Locally constant on connected sets.

Assume \(f\) is continuous on the connected set \(D\text{.}\) The following statements are equivalent.
  1. \(f\) is constant
  2. \(f\) is locally constant.
  3. \(f(D)\) is a discrete subset of \(\C\text{.}\)
