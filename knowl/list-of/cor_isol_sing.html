Corollary 1.22.6.

Assume \(z_0\) is an isolated singularity of \(f\) and \(g\text{,}\) and that \(\ord_f z_0=m\) and \(\ord_g z_0=n\) are both integers.
  1. \(\ord_{fg}z_0=\ord_f z_0+\ord_g z_0=m+n\text{.}\)
  2. \(\ord_{f+g} z_0\geq \min\{\ord_f z_0+\ord_g z_0\}\text{.}\)
  3. \(\ord_{1/f} z_0=-\ord_f z_0=-m\text{.}\)

Proof.

The proof is left as an exercise.
