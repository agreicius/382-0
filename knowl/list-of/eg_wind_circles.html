Example 1.24.2. Winding numbers of circles.

Fix an integer \(k\in \Z\text{,}\) \(z_0\in \C\text{,}\) and \(R> 0\text{,}\) and let \(\gamma(t)=z_0+Re^{kit}\text{,}\) \(t\in [0,2\pi]\text{.}\) Prove:
\begin{equation*} \chi_\gamma (w_0)=\begin{cases} k \amp \text{if } w_0\in B_R(z_0) \\ 0 \amp \text{if } w_0\in \C-\overline{B}_R(z_0) \end{cases}\text{.} \end{equation*}
