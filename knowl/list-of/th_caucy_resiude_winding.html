Theorem 1.24.7. Winding number residue theorem.

Let \(\gamma\) be a closed path, let \(U=\C-\im \gamma\text{,}\) and let \(f\) be analytic on \(U-\{z_1,z_2,\dots, z_n\}\text{,}\) where for all \(1\leq k\leq n\) is an isolated singularity of \(f\text{.}\) We have
\begin{equation} \int_\gamma f\, dz=2\pi i\sum_{k=1}^n\chi_\gamma(z_k)\res_f(z_k)\text{.}\tag{1.91} \end{equation}

Proof.
