Definition 1.17.7. Radius of convergence.

The radius of convergence of a power series \(f(z)=\sum_{n=0}^\infty a_nz^n\) is the unique extended real number \(R\in [0,\infty)\cup \{\infty\}\) satisfying properties (i)-(ii) in CorollaryÂ 1.17.6. The circle of convergence of the power series \(f\) is the set \(\{z\in \C\colon \abs{z-z_0}=R\}\text{.}\)
