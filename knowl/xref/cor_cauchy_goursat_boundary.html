Corollary 1.14.17. Principle of deformation.

Assume \(\gamma\) is a closed piecwise smooth and simple path with counterclockwise orientation, and assume \(\gamma_1, \gamma_2,\dots, \gamma_n\) are closed piecewise smooth and simple curves lying within \(\gamma\) and oriented counterclockwise. If \(f\) is holomorphic on the set \(U\) of all points lying inside \(\gamma\) and outside \(\gamma_k\) for all \(k\text{,}\) then
\begin{equation} \int_\gamma f\, dz=\sum_{k=1}^n\int_{\gamma_k}f\, dz\text{.}\tag{1.59} \end{equation}
