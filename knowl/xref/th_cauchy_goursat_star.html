Theorem 1.14.7. Cauchy-Goursat: star-shaped regions.

Assume \(U\subseteq \C\) is open and star-shaped.
  1. Closed paths.
    If \(f\) is holomorphic on \(U\text{,}\) then \(\int_\gamma f\, dz=0\) for any closed path \(\gamma\) lying in \(U\text{.}\)
  2. Antiderivatives.
    If \(f\) is holomorphic on \(U\text{,}\) then \(f\) has an antiderivative on \(U\text{.}\)

Proof.
