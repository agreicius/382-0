Corollary 1.19.3. Order of \(f\) at \(z_0\).

Let \(f\) be analytic at \(z_0\text{,}\) and let \(m\) be a positive integer. The following statements are equivalent.
  1. \(\ord_f(z_0)=m\text{.}\)
  2. We can write \(f(z)=(z-z_0)^mg(z)\text{,}\) where \(g\) is analytic at \(z_0\) and \(g(z_0)\ne 0\text{.}\)
